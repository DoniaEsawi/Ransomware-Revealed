What is the problem?¶

As you saw, there are alot of companies around the world whose data is currently publicly available on the internet. This data is very sensitive and can be used to harm the company.

Our goal is to understand cyber threats that prioritize the safety and security of our customer's sensitive information, which can help build trust and confidence in cybersecurity companies' services.

Our client, Saluslab, is a security company that has developed a tool to protect networks and companies from potential attackers.

Data Sources

Let's explore the data!

As we saw, the attckers mostly target the companies with middle revenue more that highest ones, this is perhaps is because companies with high revenue have more security protocols and are more difficult to attack, also the companies with very low revenue are not worth the effort as they are less likely to pay anyway.

A surprising fact is that the attackers mostly target the companies in the Construction industry, although their data might not be as critical as other industries like Health care for example. this might be because the construction companies are less likely to have a good security system. or because the number of companies in this industry is very high, usually.

Based on our available data, we have observed that only a small number of companies that paid the ransom are represented in our dataset. As a result, we are unable to fully support the insights we extracted regarding the distribution of industries. This limitation is due to the crawling frequency of our system, as well as the fact that it has been running stably for only one week.
