Our client, Saluslab, is asecurity company thathas developed a tool toprotect networks andcompanies from potential attackers.

What is the problem?¶

as you saw, there are alot of companies around the world whose data is currently publically available on the internet. This data is very sensitive and can be used to harm the company.

our goal is the gather insights about the ransomware attacks performed by different gangs to help SalusLab to protect their clients from such attacks.

Read the data¶

Let's explore the data!

as we saw, the attckers mostly target the companies with middle revenue more that highest ones, this is perhaps is because companies with high revenue have more security protocols and are more difficult to attack, also the companies with very low revenue are not worth the effort as they are less likely to pay anyway.

A surprising fact is that the attackers mostly target the companies in the Construction industry, although their data might not be as critical as other industries like Health care for example. this might be because the construction companies are less likely to have a good security system. or because the number of companies in this industry is very high, usually.
